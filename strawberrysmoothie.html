B and L's Strawberry Smoothie

Ingredieants

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • ½ cup skim milk
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed

Directions

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving:160 calories; protein 5.6g; carbohydrates 30.3g; fat 1.1g; cholesterol 4.9mg; sodium 71.4mg. Full Nutrition
